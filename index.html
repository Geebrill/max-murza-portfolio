About

Musician, composer, and mixing engineer with a Bachelor’s degree in Music from UC Berkeley. With a strong foundation in performance and composition, I have spent the past years refining my artistic voice while developing a professional approach to modern music production and mixing.

As a mixing engineer, I focus on creating clarity, balance, and depth within every track. My goal is to enhance the emotional impact of a song by shaping the sonic space, bringing vocals forward, strengthening rhythm sections, and making sure every element of the arrangement works together cohesively.

My experience includes live performances, ensemble collaborations, studio recordings, and independent creative work. Working both as a musician and engineer allows me to understand music from multiple perspectives, helping artists translate their ideas into polished and impactful final mixes.

I approach music not only as an art form, but also as a collaborative and strategic process that connects audiences and builds meaningful listening experiences. Driven by creativity, discipline, and innovation, I am always seeking opportunities to expand artistic boundaries and contribute to projects that demand both musical sensitivity and technical precision.

Terms

  • I always allow for as many revisions as needed. I will keep refining the mix until everyone is happy. To keep the workflow efficient, I set a time limit of 15 business days from delivery of the first pass. After that, revisions are $25 per pass. After 10 passes, revisions are $25 per pass, and after 15 passes they are $50 per pass.
  • The rate includes a final 44.1kHz 24-bit WAV master and an unmastered full-resolution mix with headroom.
  • Two alternate prints (instrumental and acapella) can be included free of charge. Additional mix versions may incur extra cost.
  • Stems can be provided for $50 per song.
  • Song length over 5:00 adds $25. Over 6:00 adds $50. Over 8:00 adds $100.
  • Track count over 75 adds $25. Over 100 adds $50. Over 150 adds $100.
  • First pass is typically delivered within 5–10 business days from booking.
  • All passes are sent with mastering applied so that once the final mix is approved, the track is ready for release.
  • Discounts may be available when booking and working on three or more songs at the same time.
  • If you’d like to hear more examples of my work, feel free to reach out through the Contact button and I’ll get back to you as soon as possible.
